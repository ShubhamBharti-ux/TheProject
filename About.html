Our Story

Fresh Fields was born from a simple idea: to make fresh, organic groceries accessible to everyone while supporting local farmers and producers.

Our Beginnings

Founded in 2015 by a group of local farmers and food enthusiasts, Fresh Fields started as a small farmers' market stall in Farm City. Our commitment to quality and sustainability quickly gained us a loyal following.

What began as a weekend market operation has grown into a thriving grocery delivery service serving the entire metropolitan area, while still maintaining our core values of freshness, quality, and community.

Our Growth

In 2025, we launched our delivery service to bring farm-fresh products directly to our customers' doors. This allowed us to expand our reach while maintaining the personal touch that made us successful.

Today, we partner with over 200 local farms and producers to offer the widest selection of organic and sustainable products in the region, all delivered with the same care we'd give to our own families.

Our Mission

At Fresh Fields, we believe everyone deserves access to fresh, healthy food. We're committed to sustainable farming practices, fair wages for our workers, and building a food system that benefits both people and the planet.
